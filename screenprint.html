P1: Screenprinting project

To start off the screenprinting project I had to brainstorm what I wanted to take a picture of, once I go that i was ready to start. I decided that i wanted to take a picture of me holding up the Puerto Rican flag to symbolize where im from. Then, I had to brainstrom the text i wanted to include in my screenprint, which were the words "From the roots" to show to not be afraid or ashamed of where you come from and to be encouraged from where your from. Once I chose that I had to take a photo of what I wanted to screen print. After I took my photo it was time to start the editing and online process, which yes was hard, but I had to push through.

Editing and Online process

The editing and online process required me to think harder then before, I had to edit my picture through an app called Adobe Photoshop. First, I put the words "From the roots" onto the the picture, then i had to change the size of everything and adjust it. After that, I had to change the gradient of the picture and made measurements. I made measurements according to the size of the frame I wanted and was going to use. I had to make my measurements carefully and exact so I would have no mistakes in the future. This ends the editing and online process, next I move on to the bulding and printing part.

Building, Printing, and painting process

To start off this process, I had to cut out my frame to the measurements i made on the computer. Once I cut it out on the machine, I had to staple my frame and staple the screen onto my frame. This stapleing process took about 2 - 3 days because of how much staples my frame needed, I got help from my classmates and i also helped my classmates with their stapleing process aswell. Once I got done stapeling everything together, it was time to print out my image. When i printed my image out, I had to cut and trim out some pieces because we had to use a special printer. Once I finished doing that, I had to start the painting process. The painting process took around 1 - 2 days because it was much easier then the stapleing process. I only chose one color to paint my image and that color was black. Once I finished painting my frame, I started to paint my box, the same screen print that was on my frame would be on my box, it was particularly easy to do. Once i finished painting my box, i folded it up and was all finished with my Screenprining Project.
